Upload a data product

For example CSVs, see the photometry and spectroscopy sample files. FITS is supported for spectra.
For Photometry, the following fields are required:

  • time - Either 'time' or 'mjd' (in MJD format), or 'jd' (in JD format).
  • magnitude
In addition, the following fields are supported:
  • error (in magnitude)
  • limit (limiting magnitude for a non-detection)
  • telescope (e.g. NTT, ZTF)
  • source
  • filter (e.g. r, V, B)
Additional columns are saved but not shown. Column names are not case-sensitive.
